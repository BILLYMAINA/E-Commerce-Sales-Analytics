# E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard

A real-world **Power BI project** analyzing e-commerce performance across customer segments, product categories, and regions. This project demonstrates end-to-end analytics, from **data cleaning in Excel** and **data processing in SQL Server** to **data modeling and visualization in Power BI**, turning raw sales data into actionable business insights.

## Project Overview

The dashboard delivers a 360° view of sales and profit trends across **Consumer**, **Corporate**, and **Home Office** segments.  
It enables stakeholders to monitor key KPIs, uncover regional trends, and evaluate category performance to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Workflow

1. **Data Cleaning – Excel** :  Removed duplicates, standardized column formats, and ensured data integrity.
2. **Data Processing – SQL Server**: Imported cleaned data into SQL Server for querying, validation, and KPI computation.
3. **Data Modeling – Power BI** :  Built relationships, created DAX measures, and optimized model performance.
4. **Visualization – Power BI** : Designed interactive dashboards with drill-downs, dynamic filters, and KPIs for quick business insight.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze overall sales, profit, and profit margins across customer segments.  
- Compare category performance (Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology).  
- Evaluate regional and state-level contribution to revenue and profit.  
- Identify best- and worst-performing products and customer groups.  
- Provide clear recommendations to improve sales strategy and profitability.

---

## Key Insights

### Overall Performance
- **Total Sales:** $11.53M | **Profit:** $1.34M | **Profit Margin:** 11.6%  
- **West Region (32%)** leads in revenue, followed by **East (28%)**.  
- **Furniture** and **Office Supplies** generate the highest sales volume.  
- **Standard Class shipping** dominates with over 60% of all orders.

## Recommendations

- **Prioritize high-demand items** in Furniture and Office Supplies.  
- **Expand marketing** in top-performing regions (West & East).  
- **Enhance delivery options** to improve customer satisfaction.  
- **Re-evaluate low-margin SKUs** to strengthen profitability.  
- **Leverage segment insights** to personalize campaigns and pricing.
